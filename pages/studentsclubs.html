STUDENTS CLUBS / SOCIETY

Of the students, by the students and for the students, this is the Directorate of Student Affairs. A student body meant for everything cultural in campus, the directorate comprises of multiple domains, clubs and houses, guided by the Director and staff members. We organize events ranging from club activities and inter house competitions to major events like Firefox, SkyScrapper, 2Furious and Bolt.
