About Department

B.Tech CSE is an engineering course which is based in various engineering disciplines such as computer engineering, mechanical engineering, communications, electronic, Software engineering etc. along with the knowledge of computer systems.

An Engineering programme which seamlessly articulates the concepts from basic science to technology with relevant practice through lab and projects for building the problem solving skills, keeping industry requirements in place. Students will develop competence in areas like programming, Database Management Systems, Networks and Security, Operating Systems, Cloud Computing etc.

Why BTECH CSE @ Miramar

CSI Professional Society Chapter: To enhance the skill, spread technical knowledge to initiate program that are targeted to produce motivated and talented individual that can be absorbed in the industry with ease.

Five Centre of Excellences: Industry collaborations with Syscom Corporations, R Systems International, Apple iOS Apps Development,Android Apps Development and SALT (School of Applied Learning and Testing) and to provide better employability to the students by providing them opportunity to apply the acquired skills independently, professionally and safely in real industrial environment in campus itself.

Department Club: THE UNIX UNION that organizes inter college event like “Tech Appear” or developing professional skills like leadership, team work, business acumen, resource generation & management among the students.

Live Projects: Providing opportunity for the students to work practically on industrial live projects and Implement their ideas as well as through project like SPARX.

Summer Internships: Excellent summer internship opportunities in various technologies such as Data Science, Block Chain, CRM etc. This is arranged by the department for every student.

Expert lectures, Workshops, Seminars: Organization of expert lectures, seminars and workshops to keep students abreast of new advancement in the field.

Vision

To be recognized as one of the focused Engineering Department imparting quality education among all the colleges at state and national level.
