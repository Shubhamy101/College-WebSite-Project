What is M.Tech (Master of Technology)?

Master of Technology (M.Tech) is the higher specialized course in the field of engineering. The duration of this course is 2 years. M.Tech provides you the insight and deep knowledge of the subjects that you have studied in your B.Tech course. Many well-known institutions run the M.Tech programme.

It forms an extension of an engineer's graduate education and provides him with an opportunity for practical implementation of engineering knowledge.An M.Tech graduate has a number of career opportunities open for him. The rapid pace of development in the country and the need for skilled engineers has meant that there are a number of vistas for M.Tech graduates.

Miramar Offers lot of M.Tech specializations to candidates. It is recommended that every candidate must take Masters in their B.tech programme since it equips them better with the requisite skills for specific domains.

Computer Science & Engineering

M.Tech. CSE is designed to offer eligible candidates the requisite skills needed in the application, research, and development activities in emerging areas of Computer Science. The course focuses on AI and AI-related applications in a distributed computing environment.

Automation & Robotics Engineering

Ideal for those who hold bachelor's degrees in the relevant branches of Engineering like Electronics and Communication, Electrical, Mechanical, Computer Science etc. and are particularly interested in designing, controlling and creating robotic systems.

Petroleum Engineering

Acquire proper training in petroleum engineering with a primary focus on exploration and production of Oil & Gas.Gain a thorough knowledge of all major technical aspects in relation to the entire hydrocarbon value-chain and the strategic techno-commercial issues.
