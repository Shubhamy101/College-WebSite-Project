HOSTEL LIFE

Separate hostels for boys and girls exist inside the campus. Hostel rooms are provided on twin/three sharing basis with all modern facilities. The hostels also have separate mess with latest Kitchen equipment and spacious dining halls to provide healthy and hygienic meals. A spacious room well ventilated designed for comfort and equipped with bed, study table and cupboards provided for the students. Each floor has facilities for toilets, bathroom, common room, recreation activities i.e. TV, indoor games and newspapers. The hostels have round the clock security and students are expected to abide by the hostel rules.

Overlooking the green pastures, hostels can accommodate up-to 600 boys and around 150 girls. It offers single- or double occupancy rooms depending on availability, student seniority and merit. Miramar provides affordable residential accommodation integrated within the campus to cultivate an environment conducive to research and promote dialogue among students, their peers, and faculty members.The residential accommodations are coupled with all facilities that include:

  • Well-furnished rooms
  • Round-the-clock electricity
  • RO water supply
  • Hot water
  • Extensive Wi-Fi connectivity across all rooms
  • Separate upgraded gymnasium for boys and girls
  • Modern Laundry facility with Electrolux machines
  • Availability of AC rooms as per the requirement of students
  • Hygienic and nutritious food
  • 24 hour security
  • Special access to the library and computer lab even after the normal working hours
