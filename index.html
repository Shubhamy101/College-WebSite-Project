Explore Now The Oppurtunies That Lie Ahead And Build Your Career

Miramar University is devoted to excellence in teaching, learning, and research, and to developing leaders in many disciplines who make a difference globally. The University has an enrollment of over 20,000 degree candidates, including undergraduate, graduate, and professional students. Miramar has more than 360,000 alumni around the world.

We empower students who want to make a difference. Our classroom academics, hands-on educational experiences and global learning adventures put students on the path toward lives and careers that will shape the world.
